| Variable                      | Description
|:----------------|:------------------------------------------------------------------

|`gender`                       | Sex of the infant (0 = female, 1 = male)
|`inborn`                       | Whether the infant was born at Duke Hospital (0 = inborn) or transferred from another facility (1 = outborn)
|`race_ethnicity`               | Combined racial/ethnic classification
|`length_of_stay`               | Total length of stay (in days) from admission to discharge or death
